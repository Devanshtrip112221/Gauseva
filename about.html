Gau Seva ka matlab hai gaayon ki seva karna. Is app ka maksad hai ki har koi aasani se gau mata ki seva kar sake.

Kaise Use Kare?

share location
Go to the home page, there you will find 'Share your location', click on it

  • App par apna location share karke aas paas ke gau shala ya stray cows dekh sakte ho.
  • Upload photo option se cow ki condition share kar sakte ho.
  • Donation ya seva ka option bhi available hoga.

Gaay ko Khana Dene ke Niyam

  • Hamesha taja chara ya gehu ka chhilka dena chahiye.
  • Plastic bags ya kachra kabhi na khilayein.
  • Paani saaf aur paryaapt matra me uplabdh karayein.

gar Gaay Bimar ho

  • Agar gaay ko bukhar (fever) ho to turant vet doctor se sampark karein.
  • Bimar cow ki photo upload karke seva group me share kar sakte ho.
  • Doctor ke aane tak saaf jagah par araam karwayen.
