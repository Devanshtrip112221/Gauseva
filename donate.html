Devotion & Service For Gau Mata

Founded in 2013 by Pujya Devi Chitralekha Ji, Gau Seva Dham is dedicated to saving and serving Gau Mata and helpless animals. Situated in Hodal, Palwal District, Haryana, the NGO operates under the World Sankirtan Tour Trust.
